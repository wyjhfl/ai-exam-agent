# 考研英语高频词汇与写作模板

## 一、高频核心词汇

### 学术论述类
- substantial: 大量的，实质性的
- comprehensive: 全面的，综合性的
- fundamental: 基本的，根本的
- inevitable: 不可避免的
- predominant: 主要的，占优势的
- unprecedented: 前所未有的
- simultaneously: 同时地
- consequently: 因此，结果

### 因果关系类
- attribute to: 归因于
- result in: 导致
- stem from: 源于
- give rise to: 引起
- contribute to: 促成
- account for: 解释，说明

### 观点表达类
- maintain: 主张
- advocate: 提倡
- contend: 认为
- assert: 断言
- emphasize: 强调
- acknowledge: 承认

## 二、大作文写作模板

### 图画作文模板（正面现象）
The picture depicts [描述图画内容], which reveals a common phenomenon in contemporary society that [揭示现象].

What accounts for this phenomenon? Several factors contribute to it. First and foremost, [原因一]. Moreover, [原因二]. Last but not least, [原因三].

In my view, this trend is generally positive/encouraging. It not only [积极影响一], but also [积极影响二]. Therefore, we should [建议措施] to further promote this positive development.

### 图画作文模板（负面现象）
As is vividly shown in the picture, [描述图画内容]. This scene reflects a worrying trend that [揭示问题].

The causes of this issue are manifold. To begin with, [原因一]. In addition, [原因二]. Furthermore, [原因三].

To address this problem, concerted efforts are needed. On the one hand, [建议一]. On the other hand, [建议二]. Only in this way can we effectively solve the problem and build a more harmonious society.

## 三、小作文写作要点

### 建议信结构
1. 自我介绍 + 写信目的
2. 具体建议（2-3条，用firstly, secondly, finally连接）
3. 表达期望 + 礼貌结尾

### 常用开头句式
- I am writing to express my concern about...
- I would like to offer some suggestions regarding...
- As a student who..., I feel obliged to...

### 常用结尾句式
- I hope you will take my suggestions into consideration.
- I look forward to seeing positive changes.
- Thank you for your attention to this matter.
